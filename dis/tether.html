Tether

is a cryptocurrency based on the value of the US dollar: 1 USDT is always equal to $ 1. it is the most popular among the cryptocurrencies that fall under the definition of stable coin, i.e. those cryptocurrencies designed to maintain a stable value over time, based on the value of other currencies or commodities such as dollars, euros, gold etc. The value of the Tether is guaranteed by collateral that equals the number of USDT in circulation, in this case in US dollars.

Technical overview

Tether is mainly used by crypto-exchanges to simulate the use of fiat motives such as USD and EUR, while avoiding all the problems related to their management (Know Your Customer). Tether uses a protocol called 'Omni Layer' which allows you to create and trade digital assets using the Bitcoin blockchain.

MONETARY POLICY

Ogni USDT in circolazione ha un corrispettivo USD su dei conti bancari. Non esiste un numero massimo di tether e questi vengono creati su richiesta.
